About Me

Hi! I'm Catie Mestayer, github username is catybyrd. I grew up in a small town in Northern Wisconsin. After becoming an adult I moved to the big city of Milwaukee. There I spent most of my career in glass manufacturing, first specializing in neon, and then moving onto scientific glassblowing.

Like a lot of people though, I found myself needing to change up my career.

I loved working with the scientists and engineers to help them improve their processes. So I started researching how I could still be of use, and I decided to become a developer!

While my background might not have obvious connections with being a developer, I believe my first hand knowledge in other fields could provide certain insights to working with customers of all types and understanding their needs.

Outside of manufacturing, I also have experience with ISO:9001 and maintaining documents in a document management system.

I am currently enrolled in the MIT xPRO Professional Certificate in Coding Course which covers the MERN Stack (MongoDB, ExpressJS, React, NodeJS), Airbnb Style Guide for JavaScript, Mozilla Developer Network for JavaScript, Docker Containers, DevOps, Cloud, CSS, and HTML.

I'm still working on where I might fit best in this industry and am excited to working on many different types of projects.

Outside of work I love to play with my dog in nature, cooking and baking, making various crafts, and of course binging on my favorite shows.
